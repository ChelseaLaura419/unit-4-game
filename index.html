Yubaba's Bathhouse Buffet

You will be given a randon number at the start of the game. There are meals below. By clicking on the meal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by eating enough to match your total score to the random number but if you eat too much and your score goes over, you lose the game and turn into a PIG!. The value of each meal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each meal.
